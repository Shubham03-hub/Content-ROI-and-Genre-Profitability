# Content-ROI-and-Genre-Profitability:-
Content ROI and Genre Profitability is a data analytics project focused on evaluating content performance across different Genres and Platforms using financial and engagement metrics.

# This dataset contains content performance data for 2023–2024, segmented by:
Year & Quarter,Series Name,Genre (Action, Drama, Comedy, Thriller),Platform (YouTube, TV, OTT)
Budget (THB),Revenue (THB),Social Mentions,Merchandise Sales (THB),Profit (THB),ROI %
Profit Margin %.
The analysis focuses on content monetization efficiency and genre-level profitability trends.

# Key Insights:-
Action + YouTube = Highest Profitability Combination
Both Action series delivered >125% ROI.
Strong correlation between Social Mentions and Revenue.
Merchandise Sales Boost Profit
Action content generated 30M THB merchandise in 2024 Q1.
Merchandise contributes significantly to profit margins.
OTT Underperformance
ROI dropped to 27.27% in 2024 Q2.
High budget but weaker revenue scaling.
Engagement Drives Revenue
Higher social mentions → Higher revenue realization.
Super Legends had highest engagement (220K) and highest ROI.

# Business Impact & Recommendations:-
Enables data-driven content investment decisions
Identifies high ROI genres
Improves budget allocation efficiency
Highlights platform-level monetization strength
# Action content on YouTube delivers the highest ROI and profitability.
